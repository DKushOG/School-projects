Personal Life

My Inspiration

My inspiration to create Hermes came from young, where I was always interested in aircraft. I built and flew radio controlled aircraft with my father since I was a kid, and am proud to be able to translate the skills I had aquired into a business model that I am passionate about

My Abilities

Before working on building Hermes, I was introduced to aeronautical engineering in Singapore Polytechnic, which got the ball rolling in terms of honing my practical skills in electronics, prototyping and building. I then moved on to UOL computer science in 2023, where my interest in Artificial Intelligence and machine learning peaked and ended up being the catalyst to me developing the automated drone service thousands of people use every day.
